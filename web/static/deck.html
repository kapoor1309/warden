The setup

Today, AI agents pay company bills.

An agent is just a small AI worker. Companies now line up a few of them to handle invoices:

  • One agent reads the invoice.
  • One agent checks it against company records.
  • One agent approves it.
  • One agent sends the money.

This is fast and cheap. But it opens a door for thieves.

The problem

A thief sends a fake invoice.

  • The invoice looks completely normal.
  • But the bank account on it has been quietly changed to the thief's account.
  • The agents do not notice. They approve it and send the money straight to the thief.

This is a real scam. Banks call it Business Email Compromise. In plain words: a fake invoice with a swapped bank account.

The gap nobody covers

The real danger is your own agent.

  • Everyone else protects one agent at a time: its input and its output.
  • Nobody watches what happens between the agents.
  • If a fake invoice tricks one of your agents, the whole team trusts it.That tricked agent is now on the thief's side, and it looks just like a normal teammate.
The solution

Warden is a security team that watches the other agents.

It sits inside the same chat room as your agents and checks every step. It has four members:

Warden, the guard Investigator, the detective Threat-Intel, the expert Enforcer, the bouncer

No money can move until Warden says it is safe.

The clever part

Why Warden cannot be fooled.

A team of AI agents

They all read the same fake invoice. One trick fools all of them. They agree, and the money goes out.

Warden

It does not trust the invoice at all. It checks the company's own records instead. The yes or no answer is plain code, not an AI guess.

“Warden checks what a correct payment should look like. So it catches brand new tricks it has never seen, with no new code.”
Built on Band

Our agents really work together on Band.

Band is the chat platform where the agents live. We use its rooms for real teamwork, not just messages:

  • When an agent needs help, it pulls another agent into the room. Agents bringing in agents.
  • When an agent is caught, the Enforcer removes it from the room.
  • If anyone joins or leaves, Warden sees it at once. A tricked agent cannot sneak in a helper or quietly slip away.
